What is BASED?

Based is a dynamic community of brazen pioneers within the TAP/Ordinals space where innovation, collaboration, and cutting-edge technology converge. Built on the foundation of creativity and shared purpose, BOT brings together builders, artists, intellectuals, entrepreneurs, and investors to drive positive change within the tap ecosystem and beyond.

Built on TAP

Founded in early 2023, Based are pioneers in the TAP Protocol ecosystem and in the Ordinals space. TAP is an OrdFi-enabling protocol for Bitcoin Ordinals. It works entirely without the use of L2 chains but utilizes tapping - a simple mechanism to verify transactions within the protocol.

The $Based Token

The BASED token represents membership to our exclusive community of builders, innovators, investors, artists, and intellectuals within the TAP/Ordinals ecosystem. With only 1000 tokens in existence, members benefit from exclusive token airdrops; NFTs; in-person events; priority access to BOT Launchpad, the BasedOnTap Index Fund, and community governance.
