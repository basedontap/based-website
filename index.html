What is BASED?

BasedOnTap, a pioneering platform natively integrated with the Bitcoin blockchain, is poised to disrupt the launchpad landscape. Leveraging this native architecture, BasedOnTap offers an array of services that address critical challenges for projects, including management, marketing, audits, and community engagement. Notably, BasedOnTap's forward-looking approach ensures secure access to liquidity for projects building on Bitcoin metaprotocols. In a realm marked by innovation, BasedOnTap represents the vanguard of the Bitcoin ecosystem, redefining the launchpad experience.

Built on TAP

Founded in early 2023, Based are pioneers in the TAP Protocol ecosystem and in the Ordinals space. TAP is an OrdFi-enabling protocol for Bitcoin Ordinals. It works entirely without the use of L2 chains but utilizes tapping - a simple mechanism to verify transactions within the protocol.

The $Based Token

The BASED token, an integral component of our ecosystem, empowers investors with exclusive benefits. As an esteemed BASED token holder, you gain privileged access to OrdiArt Airdrops, a private community of founders and artists, as well as guaranteed whitelistings and elevated staking APYs on the BasedOnTap Launchpad. These incentives serve as a testament to our commitment to enhancing the value proposition for our community. The BASED token is more than a digital asset; it is the cornerstone of our vision to enrich and strengthen the Bitcoin metaprotocols ecosystem, offering a distinctive and rewarding experience for those who choose to join us on this innovative journey.

Max Supply: 1000

Mint Process: 100%

The $BOOST Token

The BOOST token is the driving force behind the BasedOnTap Launchpad. BOOST empowers users with a dynamic mechanism for achieving Tier Levels, dictating project token allocations. By staking BOOST tokens, participants not only maximize their potential staking rewards but also gain exclusive access to IDOs on the launchpad.

Max Supply: 21 Trillion

Mint Process: 0%
